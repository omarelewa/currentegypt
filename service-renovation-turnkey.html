Renovation & Turnkey

Upgrades and handover-ready solutions with minimal operational disruption.

Overview

Renovation and turnkey delivery is where Current works inside real constraints: occupied buildings, running production lines, and fixed handover dates. We refurbish, upgrade, extend, and re-purpose existing facilities — renovating finishes, retrofitting MEP systems, and tying new structures into old ones — with works phased around your operations so disruption stays minimal. At Milita 3 we connected a new pre-engineered steel expansion to the original concrete factory, engineering specialized foundations and connections and coordinating MEP throughout, while production continued uninterrupted.

Because our crews are self-performed, we control sequencing, workmanship, and site discipline directly — critical when working metres from live operations. In-house QA/QC governs every trade, HSE protocols protect occupants and workers alike, and turnkey scopes are inspected, snagged, and handed over ready to use, as delivered on villas, the R1 development, sports halls, and the FOX9 camp.

What’s included

  • Refurbishment & upgrades
  • Phased works in live sites
  • Structural & MEP renovation
  • Turnkey delivery
  • Handover-ready completion
  • Factory repair & expansion tied into existing structures
  • Re-purposing & conversion of existing facilities
  • Pre-handover inspection & snagging

How we deliver

Step 01

Condition survey & phasing plan

We survey the existing structure and systems, map your operational constraints, and sequence the works so production and occupancy continue safely throughout.

Step 02

Engineering the interface

New work is engineered to meet old: specialized foundations, structural connections between new steel and existing concrete, and MEP tie-ins coordinated before crews mobilize.

Step 03

Self-performed execution

Our own crews execute structural, finishing, and MEP works under in-house QA/QC and strict HSE controls, with dust, noise, and shutdown windows managed around live operations.

Step 04

Snag, commission & hand over

Every space is inspected and snagged, systems are tested and commissioned, and the facility is handed over complete — ready to occupy and operate from day one.

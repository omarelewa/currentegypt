Fit-Out (Commercial & Residential)

Turnkey interior finishes delivered to spec and on time.

Overview

From shell-and-core to fully finished space, Current delivers turnkey interiors — partitions, joinery, finishes, MEP fit-out and FF&E — to brand and specification standards, with minimal disruption to surrounding operations. Our range runs from brand-standard commercial and retail interiors, such as the Calvin Klein facility fit-out, to premium landmark finishing: GRC wall panels, classic woodwork, ornate marble and wooden flooring, and GRC false ceilings that conceal MEP and HVAC services, as executed at Al Fattah El Aleem. We also deliver complete internal and external finishing packages on villas and housing.

Because our crews are self-performed, we control sequencing, workmanship and program directly — no layered subcontracting between your specification and the finished wall. In-house QA/QC inspects every trade against approved samples and mock-ups, HSE supervision runs continuously on site, and works are coordinated with live building services so handover happens once, snag-free and on schedule.

What’s included

  • Partitions & ceilings
  • Finishes & joinery
  • MEP fit-out
  • FF&E & branding
  • GRC wall panels & false ceilings concealing MEP/HVAC
  • Marble, wooden flooring & classic woodwork
  • Facade integration — GRC, marble, CNC steel, double-glazed aluminium
  • Snagging & handover

How we deliver

Step 01

Survey & scope

We survey the shell or existing space, verify as-built conditions against design intent, and lock the finishes schedule, brand standards and long-lead items before mobilising.

Step 02

Samples & mock-ups

Material samples, joinery prototypes and room mock-ups are submitted for approval — marble selections, GRC moulds, woodwork profiles — so quality is agreed before production starts.

Step 03

Self-performed execution

Our own crews run partitions, MEP fit-out, ceilings and finishes in a coordinated sequence, with in-house QA/QC inspecting each trade and HSE supervision on site throughout.

Step 04

Snag, commission & hand over

Systems are tested and commissioned, snags cleared against a joint inspection list, and the space handed over clean, documented and ready for occupation or trading.

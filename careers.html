Build a region. Build a career.

We're a self-performing contractor. We run our own crews and our own plant, and we've been building for three decades across three countries. If you're an engineer, a site hand or a partner who takes the work seriously, there's a place for you here.

Why Current

Serious work for serious builders

  • Work across Egypt, Saudi Arabia and Libya

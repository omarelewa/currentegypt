Civil Works & Earthworks

End-to-end site prep, excavation, and foundations delivered safely and efficiently.

Overview

Civil works and earthworks are where every Current project starts: taking raw or constrained ground and turning it into a platform ready to build on. Our scope runs from site clearance, bulk and detailed excavation, and general earthworks through soil improvement, enabling works, shoring, dewatering, and shallow and deep foundations. It is capability proven on real ground — soil improvement beneath the Galala villas and R1 buildings and the Port Said halls, and a 20×20 m seawater intake at Sidi Kerir excavated 15 m deep, cast with 60 cm retaining walls and leak-tested in live marine conditions.

We self-perform with our own plant, surveyors, and crews, so schedules hold and workmanship is controlled at the workface rather than pushed down a subcontracting chain. In-house QA/QC verifies compaction, levels, and concrete quality at every lift, while our HSE system governs excavation, dewatering, and confined works. From Libyan desert mobilisations with full base camps to congested urban plots, we bring the same disciplined setup to every site.

What’s included

  • Site clearance & bulk earthmoving
  • Excavation, shoring & dewatering
  • Shallow & deep foundations / piling
  • Roads, paving & hardscape
  • Drainage, utilities & backfill
  • Soil improvement & ground stabilisation
  • Enabling works & site mobilisation, including remote base camps
  • Underground utility networks, water wells & treatment-plant civil works

How we deliver

Step 01

Survey & ground assessment

We verify topographic and geotechnical data, review soil investigation reports, and define cut-fill balances, groundwater conditions, and any soil-improvement scope before a machine moves.

Step 02

Enabling works & mobilisation

Our crews establish access, hoarding, temporary utilities, and site facilities — up to full desert base camps for remote sites — with our own plant fleet deployed and inspected.

Step 03

Earthworks & foundations

Excavation, shoring, dewatering, soil improvement, and foundation works proceed under continuous QA/QC: compaction tests per layer, level checks, rebar and concrete inspections at every pour.

Step 04

Testing & handover

We prove the work before handover — plate-load and density results, as-built surveys, and where required full leak testing of water-retaining structures — documented and signed off.

Typical applications

Residential compounds & villas Industrial halls & warehouses Marine & seawater intake structures Water & sewage treatment plants Underground utility networks Remote desert facilities & base camps

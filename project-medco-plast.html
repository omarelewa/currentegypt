MEDCO Plast Factory

An industrial landmark engineered around an impressive array of 16-metre-high steel columns.

Scope

Delivered on a full EPC basis for Medco in 2016, the MEDCO Plast Factory in 6th of October City, Cairo, was designed with precision and engineering excellence from the ground up. The works combined heavy concrete foundations with a robust steel superstructure, culminating in the facility's defining feature — an array of steel columns rising a full 16 metres, giving the plant its landmark industrial silhouette.

The robust steel framework ensures long-term structural integrity while opening up an expansive, column-efficient interior — a highly functional space tailored specifically for efficient plastics manufacturing operations. Carrying the project from engineering through procurement to construction under a single EPC scope allowed fabrication and erection to proceed seamlessly, keeping quality control in one set of hands from first drawing to handover.

Service

General Contracting

Full-scope execution with rigorous QA, transparent reporting, and complete resourcing.

Overview

As main contractor, Current takes single-point responsibility for the entire build. We supply the raw materials, tools, labor, and equipment ourselves and manage full project execution — from earthworks and soil improvement through concrete structures, insulation, finishes, MEP, and landscaping to turnkey handover. Because our crews are self-performed rather than layered through subcontractors, owners get one accountable team, direct control over quality and programme, and no gaps between trades — the delivery model behind the Galala villas, the R1 buildings, and the Port Said halls.

Procurement is a genuine edge: we source locally and import directly, with in-house customs and shipping know-how that keeps critical equipment moving to site on schedule. Every project runs under formal HSE controls and in-house QA/QC, with transparent reporting that shows owners exactly where progress, resources, and risks stand at every stage.

What’s included

  • Single-point project delivery
  • Procurement & subcontractor management
  • HSE & quality management
  • Cost & programme control
  • Transparent progress reporting
  • Full resourcing — materials, tools, labor & equipment
  • Local & import sourcing with customs & shipping handling
  • Turnkey handover with testing & documentation

How we deliver

Step 01

Scope & mobilise

We review drawings, site conditions, and programme, then mobilise our own crews, equipment, and site management under a single delivery plan.

Step 02

Procure & resource

Materials and critical equipment are secured through local and import channels, with customs and shipping managed in-house to protect the schedule.

Step 03

Execute & control

Self-performed trades progress from earthworks through structure, MEP, and finishes under formal HSE controls, QA/QC inspection, and transparent weekly reporting.

Step 04

Commission & hand over

Systems are tested and commissioned, snags closed, and the completed asset handed over turnkey with full documentation.

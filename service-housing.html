Housing & Accommodation

Comfortable, durable residential solutions from single units to workforce housing.

Overview

We build homes people actually live in well — from high-quality individual residences and family accommodations to resort villa compounds, multi-storey apartment blocks and full workforce camps. Our track record spans 30 standalone villas delivered earthworks-to-turnkey at Al Galala, eight multi-storey residential buildings at R1 in the New Administrative Capital, residential concrete works in Riyadh, and a 200-staff prefabricated base camp in Libya complete with dining, kitchen, storage and laundry facilities.

Delivery is self-performed: our own crews carry the work from earthworks and structure through insulation, MEP, finishes and landscaping to turnkey handover, so quality and schedule stay under one roof. In-house QA/QC verifies every stage against specification, and our HSE system keeps sites disciplined — whether that site is a gated coastal compound or a remote desert camp.

What’s included

  • Apartment & villa construction
  • Workforce & staff accommodation
  • Compounds & communities
  • Supporting infrastructure
  • Amenities & landscaping
  • Earthworks, structure & envelope insulation
  • Full MEP — electrical, plumbing & HVAC
  • Prefabricated camps with dining, kitchen, laundry & storage

How we deliver

Step 01

Scope & site assessment

We review drawings, unit counts and site conditions — from urban plots to remote camp locations — and lock the delivery method, logistics plan and milestones.

Step 02

Earthworks & structure

Self-performed crews execute earthworks, foundations and concrete or prefabricated structures under continuous in-house QA/QC and HSE supervision.

Step 03

Envelope, MEP & finishes

Insulation, electromechanical systems, joinery and finishes proceed unit by unit on a tracked snag-and-close cycle, so quality is verified before the next trade moves in.

Step 04

Amenities & turnkey handover

Landscaping, supporting infrastructure and shared amenities are completed, systems are tested and commissioned, and units are handed over ready to occupy.

Service

Renovation & Turnkey

Upgrades and handover-ready work that keeps your operation running while we build.

Overview

Renovation and turnkey is the work we do inside real constraints. The building is usually occupied, the production line is usually running, and the handover date usually can't move. We refurbish finishes, retrofit MEP systems, extend structures and tie new work into old. Most of the difficulty isn't the building itself. It's doing it metres from live operations without shutting anything down. So we phase the work around your schedule and keep the dust, noise and shutdown windows under control. At Milita 3 we joined a new pre-engineered steel expansion onto the original concrete factory, worked out the foundations and connections, and ran the MEP through it all while the factory kept producing.

We run our own crews and plant, so we control the sequencing, the workmanship and the site discipline ourselves. That matters most when you're working next to people and machines that can't stop. Our own QA/QC checks every trade as it goes in, and our HSE rules cover your staff as well as ours. When a turnkey scope finishes, it's inspected, snagged and handed over ready to use. We've done that on villas, the R1 development, sports halls and the FOX9 camp.

What’s included

  • Refurbishment of finishes and fabric
  • Phased works in occupied and live sites
  • Structural repair, strengthening and extension
  • MEP retrofit and system upgrades
  • New steel and concrete tied into existing structures
  • Re-purposing and conversion of existing buildings
  • Full turnkey delivery
  • Pre-handover inspection and snagging

How we deliver it

We start by surveying what's already there and mapping how you use the place, then build a phasing plan that keeps you running. New work gets engineered to meet the old before anyone mobilises: foundations, the connections between new steel and existing concrete, and MEP tie-ins all worked out on paper first. Our own crews do the structural, finishing and MEP work under in-house QA/QC and strict HSE, with shutdown windows agreed with you and kept short. Everything gets snagged, tested and commissioned, and we hand over on the date we agreed so you can occupy and operate straight away.

How we deliver

Step 01

Step 02

Step 03

Step 04

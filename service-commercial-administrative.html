Service

Commercial & Administrative Buildings

Functional, modern workplaces and logistics spaces with enduring quality.

Overview

Current delivers modern commercial and administrative buildings designed for both functionality and aesthetics — headquarters and office buildings, retail and mixed-use developments, and logistics facilities engineered around how the asset will actually operate. Scope runs from structure and envelope through facade, interiors and common-area finishes, with logistics buildings fitted for climate-controlled storage, loading docks and material-handling systems, as delivered for Reebok’s distribution facility and the Nasita warehouse complex, custom-designed to modern industrial standards.

Every scope is executed by our own self-performed crews under in-house QA/QC and a strict HSE regime, giving clients direct control over quality, sequencing and pace. That control shows on extreme schedules: Vodafone Square, a landmark brand structure, went from design in one week to a finished build in three — the same disciplined delivery we bring to conventional office and logistics programmes across Egypt, KSA and Libya.

What’s included

  • Office & corporate buildings
  • Retail & mixed-use
  • Warehouse & logistics

  • Material-handling & racking coordination
  • Fast-track landmark & brand structures

How we deliver

Step 01

Brief & functional design

We align the building with its operation — workspace layouts, storage throughput, dock counts, brand identity — and lock a design and construction programme, compressed when the schedule demands it.

Step 02

Structure & envelope

Self-performed crews execute foundations, frame and envelope — concrete or steel — with facade systems sequenced to weather-tight the building early and open up interior trades.

Step 03

Fit-out & systems

Interiors, common-area finishes and building services are installed under in-house QA/QC; logistics assets receive climate control, dock equipment and material-handling coordination.

Step 04

Commissioning & handover

Systems are tested and commissioned, snags closed against our QA/QC records, and the building handed over with documentation ready for immediate occupation and operation.

Service

Design-Build

Single-source delivery that compresses schedules and reduces change orders.

Overview

Design-build puts architectural and structural design, engineering planning, material procurement, and full construction execution under one contract — and one accountable team. Backed by an in-house engineering consultancy with 35+ years of architectural and structural design expertise across concrete and steel, we take projects from feasibility studies, BOQs, and tender documentation straight into execution. Because design and construction overlap instead of running in sequence, schedules compress and the change orders that plague design-bid-build largely disappear.

Delivery is self-performed: the same crews that build our civil, structural, and MEP scopes execute the design our engineers produce, with in-house QA/QC verifying every stage against the drawings and HSE supervision embedded on site from day one. On projects like Nasita, we carried the full arc — from initial design through final construction — as a single point of responsibility.

What’s included

  • Concept-to-handover delivery
  • Integrated design management
  • Value engineering
  • Fast-track scheduling
  • Single point of accountability
  • Architectural & structural design (concrete and steel)
  • Feasibility studies, BOQs & tender documentation
  • Material procurement & supply-chain management

How we deliver

Step 01

Define & validate

We start with your brief and site: feasibility studies, concept design options, and early quantity take-offs that fix scope and constraints before detailed design begins.

Step 02

Design & engineer

Our in-house consultancy develops architectural and structural design for concrete or steel, applying value engineering while the drawings are still live — where changes cost least.

Step 03

Procure & mobilize

Long-lead materials are procured against the design as it matures, and self-performed crews mobilize on early work packages while later packages are still being detailed.

Step 04

Build & hand over

Construction proceeds under one contract with in-house QA/QC verification at every stage and HSE supervision on site, closing out with as-builts, testing, and a clean handover.

About Current

The execution capacity of a large contractor. The agility of a focused team.

Current is an EPC contractor delivering industrial, commercial, and infrastructure projects across Egypt and the GCC — planning precisely, procuring intelligently, and building safely, so clients get on-time handovers and quality that passes first time.

Who we are

An EPC partner trusted by blue-chip operators and public agencies

We offer comprehensive Engineering, Procurement, and Construction services — end-to-end delivery from concept to commissioning, covering detailed design, global material sourcing, and expert on-site construction management. Our track record includes process buildings, factories, power and utilities, and landmark institutional projects.

Operating across Egypt, Saudi Arabia, and Libya, we combine deep local market understanding with international standards — delivering efficiently within diverse regulatory and cultural landscapes for energy majors, ministries, and private developers.

Our journey

Three decades of building momentum

1993

Genesis of Current, as the co-founders began delivering extensive construction projects in Egypt.

2005

Expanded operations into Libya, entering the burgeoning oil & gas sector.

2014

Expanded into Algeria's oil & gas sector — civil works, construction, and project management services.

2015

The Egyptian branch experienced rapid growth, executing numerous large-scale projects.

2021

Current Egypt officially established, formalizing the co-founders' extensive experience.

2024

Expanded into Gulf markets, establishing Current KSA in Saudi Arabia.

2025

Established Continental Construction & Supply (Egypt), entered the Libyan market, targeting regional construction leadership.

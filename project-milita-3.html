Milita Egypt — Factory 3

A modern steel expansion seamlessly integrated with a live concrete factory — delivered with zero interruption to production.

Scope

In 2024, as main contractor, we carried out the repair and expansion of Milita Egypt's existing factory in the Duty Free Zone, Alexandria — a project defined by one non-negotiable condition: production could not stop. Advanced engineering solutions connected new pre-engineered steel buildings with the original concrete structures, requiring specialized foundations, precise structural connections between steel and concrete, and MEP systems coordinated across old and new zones without disrupting the live plant.

Every phase was sequenced around the factory's operating schedule, maintaining full operational continuity from first mobilization to handover. The expansion was delivered on schedule, optimizing workflow efficiency across the enlarged facility and positioning Milita for a significant increase in manufacturing output.

Challenge

Expand and repair a fully operational factory — joining new steel structures to aging concrete buildings with zero interruption to production.

Solution

Specialized foundations, engineered steel-to-concrete structural connections, and phased MEP coordination sequenced around the plant's live operating schedule.

Result

On-schedule delivery of an integrated, expanded facility with improved workflow efficiency and capacity for increased manufacturing output.

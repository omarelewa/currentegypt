Current is a construction-services firm delivering civil, structural, electromechanical and turnkey works across Egypt, Saudi Arabia and Libya.

Who we are

A self-performing contractor trusted by the region’s biggest names

For over three and a half decades, Current has delivered end-to-end construction for energy majors, multinationals, and government bodies. We mobilise our own crews and plant, so quality and schedule stay in our hands — which is why 82% of our work comes from clients who hire us again.

From oilfield-services facilities for Halliburton to landmark public buildings and brand fit-outs, our range spans civil and earthworks, concrete and steel, electromechanical systems, general contracting, fit-out, housing and turnkey delivery.

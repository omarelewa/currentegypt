EMC — Reebok Storage

A specialized distribution facility — climate-controlled storage, loading docks, and modern warehouse-management infrastructure.

Scope

For Elite Merchandising Company (EMC), Current delivered a specialized distribution facility in the Industrial Area of 6th of October City as a complete EPC turnkey contract, completed in 2023. The building programme centred on pre-engineered steel structures housing climate-controlled storage areas, purpose-built loading docks, and integrated material-handling systems — the physical backbone of a modern apparel distribution operation.

Current carried the project from design through construction under a single point of responsibility, incorporating warehouse-management infrastructure, security systems, and logistics layouts engineered for efficient inbound, storage, and dispatch flows. The result is a facility that directly supports EMC's distribution operations with the throughput, environmental control, and operational discipline a global brand demands.

Challenge

Deliver a climate-controlled distribution hub with loading docks and material-handling systems to global brand standards, on a single turnkey contract.

Solution

Full EPC delivery — pre-engineered steel structures, warehouse-management infrastructure, security systems, and efficiency-driven logistics layouts from design through construction.

Result

A modern, fully equipped storage and distribution facility handed over in 2023, supporting EMC's day-to-day distribution operations.

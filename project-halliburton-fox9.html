Halliburton — FOX9 Base Camp

A 250,000 m² living and industrial hub in the Libyan desert — EPC turnkey delivery to international oil & gas standards, from prefabricated housing to a 10,000 m² crane-equipped workshop.

Scope

A complete living and industrial hub for Halliburton operations, spanning 250,000 m² in the heart of the Libyan desert. The base camp accommodated 200 staff with prefabricated housing, dining, kitchen, storage, and laundry facilities — alongside an industrial complex of over 20,000 m² of specialized facilities.

Its centrepiece: a 10,000 m² workshop built with pre-engineered insulated steel structures — overhead cranes, drive-through maintenance bays, automated roll-up doors, and a double-floor design housing testing laboratories and administrative offices. Infrastructure works included drilling water wells, water and sewage treatment plants, an extensive underground electrical and piping network, and a 2,000 m perimeter of 4 m-high double-protected fencing.

Challenge

Deliver an oilfield-services facility to international oil & gas standards on a controlled, safety-critical site.

Solution

Self-performed civil and supporting MEP works with strict HSE and QA/QC under client and EPC oversight.

Result

Facility built to specification and handed over ready for operations.

Process & Industrial Buildings

Mission-critical plants and facilities engineered for continuous operations.

Overview

We build the facilities that keep industrial operations running: control centers and substations, workshops, warehouses, testing laboratories and process buildings engineered for continuous duty. Our scope spans plant civil and structural works, equipment foundations, pipe racks and industrial enclosures — delivered by our own crews, with in-house QA/QC and a permit-to-work HSE regime built for live plant environments. We coordinate directly with process and oil & gas clients and their EPC partners from early engineering through mechanical completion.

The portfolio shows the range: a 7,500 m² pre-engineered insulated steel facility for Schlumberger with 250 mm RC slabs, overhead cranes, drive-through maintenance bays and roll-up doors; 16 m-high column halls at MEDCO; double-floor laboratory designs; and factory expansions like Milita 3, executed against live production with zero interruption to the client's output.

What's included

  • Plant civil & structural works
  • Equipment foundations & plinths
  • Pipe racks & supports
  • Industrial sheds & enclosures
  • Site utilities & infrastructure
  • Pre-engineered insulated steel buildings with heavy-duty RC slabs
  • Overhead crane runways, maintenance bays & roll-up doors
  • Live-plant expansions with zero production interruption

How we deliver

Step 01

Scope & constructability review

We study process requirements, equipment loads and operational constraints with your engineering team — crane capacities, clear heights, slab loading and interfaces with live production — before a single drawing is issued for construction.

Step 02

Engineering & method statements

Foundations, structural steel and enclosures are detailed for continuous-operation duty. Method statements, HSE plans and permit-to-work procedures are agreed with plant operations up front.

Step 03

Self-performed execution

Our own crews execute civil, structural and enclosure works with in-house QA/QC at every hold point — from 250 mm RC slabs and crane runways to 16 m-high column halls — sequenced so adjacent production never stops.

Step 04

Testing, handover & support

Systems are inspected, load-tested and documented for handover. We close out punch lists fast and stay available through commissioning and early operations.

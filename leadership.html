Leadership

The people accountable for your delivery.

A leadership team blending military-grade governance, decades of oil & gas delivery, and disciplined engineering — accountable to every client, on every project.

Chairman

Maj. Gen. Mohamed Abdelaziz

Major General Mohamed Abdelaziz brings distinguished military leadership experience to guide Current's operations across Egypt, Libya, and Saudi Arabia. His strategic command background has honed exceptional capabilities in managing complex, large-scale organizations and driving them toward sustained success.

As Chairman, he sets the company's strategic direction — rigorous governance and financial oversight, comprehensive project management, stakeholder engagement, and proactive market expansion — while championing corporate social responsibility and robust risk management frameworks.

Chief Executive Officer

Eng. Mohamed Ragab

Leading Current's strategic operations since 2020, Eng. Ragab brings over 18 years of experience spanning oil & gas, manufacturing, and civil infrastructure — orchestrating multi-site industrial programs across Egypt and the GCC, from front-end planning and procurement through QA/QC, HSE compliance, and final handover.

His international career includes oil & gas facilities in Libya, Sonatrach/Medgaz pipeline-related plants in Algeria, manufacturing operations in Turkey, and multidisciplinary contracts in Egypt. Under his leadership, Current has delivered the EMC2, EMC5, EMC7, and EMC8 facilities and, since 2023, critical infrastructure packages in Riyadh in coordination with government authorities.

Construction Director

Eng. Mohamed Shelbaya

Eng. Shelbaya's strength lies in seamlessly integrating practical site execution with technical-office capability — advanced planning, comprehensive contract administration, and value engineering that translates complex drawings into practical construction solutions.

He orchestrates complete project lifecycles — design coordination, procurement, cost control, site supervision, QA/QC, and HSE compliance — cultivating high-performance site teams across factories, warehouses, administrative buildings, and infrastructure packages.

Engineering Consultant

Dr. Ahmed Baibars

Dr. Baibars, General Manager of ALNOOR Engineering Design & Consultant, brings over 35 years of expertise in architectural and structural design for concrete and steel projects throughout Egypt — leading multidisciplinary teams from design through tender documentation, feasibility, and on-site supervision to international standards.

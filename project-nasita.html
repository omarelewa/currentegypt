Nasita Factory

A comprehensive warehouse complex — multiple facilities of 3,500 m² each, delivered end-to-end from design to construction.

Scope

For Nasita, Current delivered a complete warehouse complex in the Industrial Area of 6th of October City in 2018 — a full EPC engagement covering design, engineering, procurement and construction. Our teams carried each facility from the first architectural and structural drawings through engineering planning, material procurement and full site execution, delivering multiple warehouses of 3,500 m² each under a single, continuous programme.

Every warehouse was custom-designed to modern industrial standards: efficient floor layouts that maximise usable storage volume, robust structural systems sized for heavy racking and handling loads, and practical operational features — loading access, circulation and services — configured for diverse storage and logistics requirements. The end-to-end delivery model gave the owner one accountable partner from concept to handover.

Challenge

Deliver multiple 3,500 m² warehouse facilities as one coordinated complex, each fit for different storage and logistics uses.

Solution

A single EPC programme — architectural and structural design, engineering planning, procurement and construction executed end-to-end by one team.

Result

A modern warehouse complex built to current industrial standards, with efficient layouts and robust structures ready for immediate operation.

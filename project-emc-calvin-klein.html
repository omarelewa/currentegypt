EMC — Calvin Klein Factory

A state-of-the-art manufacturing facility for the fabric industry — a 250-ton steel structure spanning 4,500 m² across two floors.

Scope

For Elite Merchandising Company (EMC), Current delivered a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in the Industrial Area of 6th of October City on a full EPC turnkey basis. The building — a 250-ton steel structure enclosing 4,500 m² over two floors — was purpose-designed for fabric-industry operations, with structural grids, floor loadings, and services routing engineered around the specialized machinery the production lines demand.

Executed between 2018 and 2019, the contract ran from earthwork through steel erection, finishes, complete MEP systems, and landscaping — a single-point delivery that kept design, procurement, and construction under one accountable team. The facility was handed over on time, exceeding the project's Quality and HSE standards.

Challenge

Engineer a two-floor, 4,500 m² production building around the loads, vibration, and services demands of specialized fabric-industry machinery.

Solution

A full EPC turnkey delivery — earthwork, a 250-ton steel structure, finishes, MEP systems, and landscaping — coordinated by one integrated team.

Result

The facility was delivered on schedule in 2019, exceeding Quality and HSE standards and ready for production fit-out from day one.
